Weather Project

Weather refers to the atmospheric conditions in a specific location at a particular time. It encompasses various elements such as temperature, humidity, precipitation (rain, snow, sleet), wind speed and direction, air pressure, and cloud cover. Weather can change rapidly and is influenced by factors like geographical location, altitude, and proximity to bodies of water. Meteorologists study weather patterns to provide forecasts that help people plan their activities and stay safe during extreme weather events.
